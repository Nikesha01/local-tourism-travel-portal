Anuradhapura




Anuradhapura is one of the ancient capitals of Sri Lanka and a UNESCO World Heritage Site,
renowned for its rich history, sacred temples, and magnificent ruins. It is one of the most
important cultural and religious destinations
in the country, attracting pilgrims and tourists from around the world.
The city is home to the sacred Sri Maha Bodhi, a sapling of the tree under which
Lord Buddha attained enlightenment.Visitors can explore ancient stupas such as
Ruwanwelisaya, Jetavanaramaya, and Abhayagiriya,
while learning about Sri Lanka’s glorious past. Anuradhapura offers a unique blend of history,
spirituality, and architectural wonders, making it an unforgettable destination
for travelers seeking cultural experiences.











3-Day Travel Itinerary

Day 1 – Sacred City Exploration

  • Arrive in Anuradhapura
  • Visit Sri Maha Bodhi
  • Explore Ruwanwelisaya Stupa
  • Evening visit to Isurumuniya Temple

Day 2 – Ancient Ruins & Heritage

  • Visit Jetavanaramaya
  • Explore Abhayagiriya Monastery Complex
  • Visit Samadhi Buddha Statue
  • Discover the ancient reservoirs (wewas)

Day 3 – Culture & Relaxation

  • Visit local museums
  • Explore traditional markets
  • Enjoy Sri Lankan cuisine
  • Departure
