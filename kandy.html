KANDY




Kandy is the cultural capital of Sri Lanka, surrounded by green hills, misty mountains, and a peaceful lake.
It is one of the most visited cities in the country, famous for its sacred
temples, natural beauty, and rich traditions. The city is home to the sacred Temple of the Sacred
Tooth Relic, one of the most important Buddhist sites in the world. Visitors can enjoy relaxing walks around Kandy Lake
and explore the beautiful Royal Botanical Gardens Peradeniya filled with exotic plants and orchids.
Kandy is also famous for the vibrant cultural festival Kandy Esala Perahera.
