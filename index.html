KANDY

Kandy is a beautiful hill city
in Sri Lanka famous for the Temple
of the Tooth Relic scenic Kandy Lake
and rich cultural heritage. It is one
of the country's most popular
tourist destinations.







NUWARAELIYA

Nuwara Eliya is a charming hill
town in Sri Lanka known
for its cool climate lush
tea plantations beautiful gardens
and scenic mountain views.
It is often called "Little England"
because of its colonial architecture
and atmosphere.



GALLE

Galle is a historic coastal
city in Sri Lanka
famous for the UNESCO-listed
Galle Fort, beautiful beaches
colonial architecture and
stunning ocean views.
It is one of the country's
most popular tourist destinations.





ELLA

Ella is a scenic mountain
town in Sri Lanka
known for its breathtaking
views, lush greenery
waterfalls, and hiking trails.
It is famous for attractions
like Nine Arches Bridge
and Little Adam's Peak.





ANURADHAPURA

Anuradhapura is an ancient
city in Sri Lanka
famous for its historic temples
sacred stupas, and rich
Buddhist heritage. It is
home to the sacred
Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi and
many well-preserved archaeological sites.
