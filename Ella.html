ELLA

Ella is a charming mountain village located in the Badulla District of Sri Lanka, surrounded by lush green hills, tea plantations,
and breathtaking waterfalls. It is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the country, known for its scenic beauty, cool climate, and relaxing atmosphere.
Visitors can enjoy stunning views from Little Adam’s Peak, hike to the famous
Ella Rock, and admire the iconic Nine Arches Bridge surrounded by dense greenery.
The area is also home to beautiful waterfalls such as Ravana Falls, making it a
perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.
Ella offers a unique blend of natural beauty, outdoor activities, and peaceful
surroundings that attract travelers from around the world.
