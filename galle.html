GALLE




Galle is a beautiful coastal city located in the southern province of Sri Lanka.
It is famous for its historic Galle Fort, stunning beaches, colonial architecture, and rich cultural heritage.
The city is home to the UNESCO World Heritage Site Galle Fort, built by the Portuguese
and later expanded by the Dutch. Visitors can enjoy walking along the fort walls, exploring charming streets,
and admiring the iconic Galle Lighthouse.
Galle also offers breathtaking ocean views, beautiful beaches such as Unawatuna,
and a relaxing atmosphere perfect for travelers seeking history and nature.











3-Day Travel Itinerary

Day 1 – Historic Galle Fort

  • Arrive in Galle
  • Explore Galle Fort
  • Visit Galle Lighthouse
  • Walk along the fort walls during sunset

Day 2 – Beaches & Nature

  • Relax at Unawatuna Beach
